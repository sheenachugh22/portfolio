About Me

Product Manager for E2E Quality

ISTQB® Advanced Level Test Manager (ALTM) certified Product and Quality leader with 9+ years of experience driving end-to-end quality for customer-facing software. I specialize in owning product outcomes, defining E2E test strategies, and partnering with Engineering and QA to ship reliable, measurable customer experiences.

My career has progressed from QA Engineer to Senior QA Consultant, Product Manager / QA Lead, and Product Manager – E2E Testing. This mix of hands-on testing, automation, and product ownership helps me translate business goals into clear requirements, acceptance criteria, and quality gates that engineering teams can execute on.

My strength lies in combining product thinking with deep E2E and automation expertise in Selenium C#, Playwright JavaScript, SoapUI, Postman, JMeter, and CI/CD—enabling me to design traceable E2E coverage and quality gates across web, mobile, and services.
